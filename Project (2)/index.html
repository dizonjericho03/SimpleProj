About

SOOO PINOY is a website where you can see and learn all about Filipino Cuisines. No matter where you are or who you are you can still enjoy the Filipino food cultures. We are a dedicated team who work on this site composing of foodies and chefs who are passionate about the Filipino Cuisines. We promote our culture to other people and Filipinos to learn more about the food they ate. Spreading the love about Filipino cuisine is our main goal not only in the Philippines but to all parts of the world.
